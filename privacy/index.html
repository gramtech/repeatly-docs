Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 February 2026

Repeatly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Repeatly iOS app ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Data you create in the App. All tasks, categories, settings, and other content you create are stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

Purchase information. If you subscribe to Repeatly Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple via the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat may collect anonymised identifiers and entitlement data. Please see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

Crash and diagnostic data. If you have opted in to share analytics with Apple (via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements), Apple may share anonymised crash reports and usage data with us through App Store Connect. We use this solely to fix bugs and improve the App.

2. Data Storage and Security

Your task data is stored exclusively on your device. It is not uploaded to any server and is not accessible to us. Data is protected by your device's built-in security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID) and iOS data protection APIs.

If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted from your device.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — payment processing and subscription management.
  • RevenueCat — subscription status verification and in-app purchase management.
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) — local notifications are scheduled on-device and do not pass through our servers. No personal data is sent to APNs.

We do not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or social media trackers.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

5. Your Rights

Because your data is stored only on your device, you have full control over it at all times:

  • Access and portability — you can view all your data directly in the App.
  • Deletion — delete any task or category in the App, or delete the App entirely to remove all data.
  • Subscription management — manage or cancel your Pro subscription at any time via iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions.

If you reside in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you may have additional rights under the GDPR or UK GDPR, including the right to access, rectify, or erase personal data held about you by RevenueCat or Apple. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
